Our Story

About MTARISE CONSTRUCTION LTD

Family-run. Professionally delivered. Genuinely passionate about building better homes.

Est. 2026Dublin, Ireland

Who We Are

Building Trust,
One Project at a Time

MTARISE CONSTRUCTION LTD was founded in 2026 by Avtandil Bitsadze — a qualified tradesperson with 9 years of hands-on residential construction experience and an unwavering commitment to quality.

What started as a small team has grown into one of the most trusted residential construction companies in the Dublin area, with over 500 successfully completed projects.

We are a fully insured, registered company with a team of directly employed and carefully vetted subcontractors — all sharing our commitment to professional standards.
