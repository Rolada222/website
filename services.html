What We Do

Our Services

From full renovations to specialist trades — everything your home project needs, delivered by one trusted team.

🏗️

Residential Construction

We build new residential homes and carry out complete builds from groundworks to finish. Our experienced team manages the full construction process, coordinating all trades and ensuring the highest standards at every stage.

What's included:

  • Full project management from planning to handover
  • Groundworks, foundations and structural builds
  • Blockwork, brickwork and masonry
  • Roofing, insulation and weatherproofing
  • Internal fit-out including flooring and plastering
  • Final snagging, inspection and client sign-off
Ideal for: New builds, self-builds, and complete residential developments.

Home Renovation

Transform your existing home with a full renovation. We handle everything from structural alterations and rewiring to plastering and decoration, breathing new life into properties of any age.

What's included:

  • Full structural and cosmetic refurbishment
  • Internal layout changes and wall removal
  • Complete replastering and skimming
  • Flooring installation (wood, tile, carpet)
  • Electrical and plumbing upgrades
  • Full decoration and finishing throughout
Ideal for: Period homes, pre-sale refurbishments, and complete interior overhauls.

House Extensions

Add valuable living space to your home with a professionally designed and built extension. We handle single-storey, double-storey and wraparound extensions, managing planning, build, and fit-out.

What's included:

  • Planning and architectural drawings assistance
  • Foundation and structural construction
  • Roof construction and weatherproofing
  • Windows, doors, and glazed elements
  • Internal plumbing and electrical integration
  • Full internal fit-out and decoration
Ideal for: Growing families needing extra bedrooms, larger kitchens, or home offices.

Attic Conversions

Unlock the hidden potential of your attic space. We convert unused loft areas into fully habitable rooms — bedrooms, home offices, playrooms — with proper insulation, lighting, and access.

What's included:

  • Structural assessment and load-bearing upgrades
  • Roof light and dormer window installation
  • Spray foam and rigid board insulation
  • Staircase design and installation
  • Electrical, lighting and heating integration
  • Full plastering, flooring and decoration
Ideal for: Homeowners wanting an extra bedroom, en-suite, or home office without building outward.

Upgrade your bathroom to a luxury retreat. From full strip-outs to designer tile work and freestanding baths, our bathroom specialists deliver stunning results on time and to budget.

What's included:

  • Full strip-out and waterproofing of wet areas
  • Plumbing and drainage installation
  • Tiling — walls, floors and feature walls
  • Sanitary ware supply and fitting
  • Underfloor heating installation
  • Electrical: shaver points, heated mirrors, lighting
Ideal for: Outdated bathrooms, en-suite additions, and full wet-room conversions.

The kitchen is the heart of the home. We renovate kitchens of all sizes — from layout redesign and structural changes to bespoke cabinetry installation, tiling, and appliance integration.

What's included:

  • Kitchen layout planning and design assistance
  • Supply and fit of kitchen units and worktops
  • Tiling to splashbacks and floors
  • Plumbing for sink, dishwasher and appliances
  • Electrical: sockets, lighting, cooker circuits
  • Painting, coving and final finish
Ideal for: Full kitchen replacements, open-plan kitchen-diner projects, and layout overhauls.
