Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions we receive from homeowners.

Quotations & Pricing

Yes, completely free and with no obligation. We visit your property, assess the scope of work, and provide a detailed written quotation — all at no cost to you. We believe you should know exactly what you're paying for before making any commitment.

Following our site visit, you'll receive a detailed written quotation within 5 working days. For smaller jobs, we can often provide this within 48 hours.

Never. Our quotations are fully detailed and transparent. We list every element of the work and every material included. If additional work is discovered during a project, we always discuss and agree it with you in writing before proceeding — there are no surprises.

We typically work on a stage-payment basis, where payments are tied to project milestones. This protects you as a client and ensures you only pay for work that has been completed to your satisfaction. Full payment terms are outlined in your written contract before any work begins.

Project & Timeline

Timelines vary greatly depending on the scope of work. A bathroom renovation typically takes 1–2 weeks. A kitchen renovation 2–4 weeks. A full house renovation can take 3–6 months. We provide a detailed project schedule before work begins and keep you updated throughout.

This depends on the scope of your project. For partial renovations (kitchen or bathroom only), most clients stay in the property. For full-house renovations, we recommend temporary alternative arrangements for the main construction phase. We always discuss this with you at the planning stage.

We take the cleanliness of your home seriously. We use dust sheeting throughout, clean up at the end of every working day, and properly segregate waste. All skips and waste materials are managed and disposed of responsibly.

Insurance & Guarantees

Yes. We carry full public liability insurance (€6.5m) and employer's liability insurance. Certificates can be provided on request. All subcontractors we use are also vetted for appropriate insurance.

Yes. All completed work carries a 12-month workmanship guarantee. If any issue arises from our work within this period, we return to rectify it at no additional charge — promptly and professionally.

Absolutely. Our electricians are RECI registered and our plumbers are RGII certified. All electrical and gas works are fully certified and appropriate certification documents are provided upon completion.

Planning & Permissions

It depends on the nature of the work. Many extensions and renovations fall under Exempted Development in Ireland and don't require planning permission, but this is assessed on a case-by-case basis. We'll advise you clearly at the site visit stage and can refer you to trusted architects where planning is required.

While we're a construction company rather than an architectural practice, we work closely with a network of trusted architects and engineers who can assist with designs, structural calculations, and planning applications. We can facilitate the entire process for you.
